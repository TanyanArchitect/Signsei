# ✨ Signsei - Auto Signature Drawer

A small, fun (and realistically, practically useless outside of gaming) tool that automatically controls your mouse to draw beautiful signatures on your screen.

## 📖 The Origin Story
Why did I make this? It all started on a beautiful day when the Magical banner opened in Blue Archive. As usual, I went to gacha after a long week of saving Pyroxenes. 

My gacha ritual: Pray -> Hit Roll -> Arona slaps the envelope in my face -> Sign the name of the student I want.

Here is the problem: "Suzumi" is a pretty long name to draw with a mouse. Anyone who signs frequently in this game knows that if you hesitate or slow down even for a split second while drawing, the game will just skip the rest of your signature. Naturally, I failed 5 or 6 times before I could draw a proper "Suzumi". (Luckily, I got both Suzumi and Reisa in 200 pulls, though I had to use 200 recruitment points to spark the second Suzumi).

Anyway, after Arona drained my 24,000 Pyroxenes, I sat there wondering: *"Is there a software that can control my mouse to write text instead of me doing it manually?"*

In the real world, a tool like this is completely useless. If people want text, they just type it. If they want beautiful typography, they use Paint or design software. Nobody needs to auto-draw text on their Desktop. 

But my personality is like this: If I feel annoyed because I can't do something perfectly, I have to create a tool to serve me immediately. That's how this tool was born.

## 🚀 Features
- **Custom Fonts:** Comes with a built-in gorgeous font (*Momo Signature*) perfect for signing, or you can integrate and use any font already installed on your PC.
- **Custom Text:** Input whatever text you want the mouse to draw.
- **Adjustable Drawing Frame:** Freely adjust the shape, size, position, and rotation angle of the bounding box to fit any screen area.
- **Hotkey Trigger:** Press your custom hotkey, wait 1-2 seconds for the tool to process the text, and watch your mouse automatically drag and draw the content on your screen.
- **Standalone & Ready to Use:** Everything is packaged. No need to install Python or any extra libraries that give you a headache. Just download, install, and run.

## 🎮 How to Use
1. Go to the [Releases](../../releases) page and download the latest setup file.
2. Install and open the program.
3. Enter your text and select a font.
4. Adjust the frame to match the signature area in your game (or anywhere on your screen).
5. Press the Hotkey, switch to your target window, and let the tool do the magic!
